About Me

I'm Gaia - my career started in people operations, and currently I'm the people partner for product & engineering teams at Ably.

My day to day work consists of catching up with our product & engineering team members to get an understanding of how their time at Ably is going, partnering with team leads & enabling them to successfully manage their teams, and employee lifecycle events - from onboarding through to offboarding. I also have the opportunity to take on projects, like updating our parental leave policy, and (my favorite) nerding out in complex Google Sheets whenever possible.

When I'm not working on Ably things, I love getting stuck into various hobbies - from creative writing, to embroidery, to making pizza from scratch, and recently I've started learning to code. Which is why this page exists. :D
